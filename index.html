Our Services

We offer Engineering, Procurement, Installation and Commissioning, Service and Maintenance. We don’t only sell you a product! We engineer it! We discuss with you, understand what you want, we present you the pros and cons, we offer you options and you choose. Then we calculate and draw, then we come back to you… We offer you a full set of documentation and we get it approved by authorities. Then we supply and install it for you… we test, commission and we start it up! Then we monitor it to prove that it works as promised. Then we take care of it, provide service and spare for its entire life of the system.
